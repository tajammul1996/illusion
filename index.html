Toughened Glass

Fabricating materials and toughened glasses with well experienced and technically sound professionals to meet the perfect tailor made end product.

Patch Fitting

Patch fittings are a frameless glass concept, which evolved as modern interior and exterior design option for commercial and residential projects. Patch fittings concepts are a collaboration of toughened glass and glass architectural hardware.

Spider Fitting

Spider fittings can be customized for various designed buildings with the help of Spider fittings hardware, toughened glass and various other components involved in the concepts. Spider fittings concepts provides the entire necessary requirement in terms of safety and security for the end users.

Sliding Sensor Door

Sliding Sensor Doors are the best solution available till date with many designing options available for the architecture and interior designers. Sliding Sensor Doors are one of the premier replacement for all other partition works. Apart from wall glass partition, glass partition can be also used as roof partition and floor partition works involving various hardware designs and models to suit almost all the dream designs. In near future glass partition work will dominate all rest of the partition work and the complete building can be glass made with the emerge of glass partition concept

Structural Glazing

Meeting structural glazing performance standards around the world, structural glazing sealants from Illusion enable architects to continue to enhance their building designs, contributing to visually stunning glass façades.

Shower Cubicles

Explore stylishly designed Shower enclosures and Panels with different door options from various brands. We also provide a customised cost efficient solution to cater your needs.

ACP Cladding

Decorative ACP Cladding is popular for its Exterior works and use in false ceilings, signage and partitions. The uniqueness of this form of decorative ACP cladding is, although it is light in weight but is sturdy in construction. These are widely used in designing transient structures such as trade show booths. The decorative ACP cladding is corrosion resistant, require less maintenance, have excellent shine and is high on durability.

Fall Ceiling

Everyone loves a great first impression and the same applies to our home, office, etc. Browse through our array of classy, cheerful, adventurous and artistic ceiling designs for inspiration. After all, great décor is the first step to an admirable first impression.

Aluminium partition

Aluminum partitions, unlike glass or wall partitions, save a lot of space by their width. They may not blend as good as a glass or a cement barrier, but they are indeed strong enough to support the room. Office partitions of any sort are better than making full and permanent walls.

UPVC Windows

uPVC stands for Unplasticised Poly Vinyl Chloride, and is also known as vinyl windows. It is a rigid, extremely durable and long lasting form of PVC used in the building industry.

SS Railings

Stainless steel (SS) railing systems have that modern look making them a great way to personalize your plain room or office space. You can use these railings to build a deck or you can use them for an exterior or interior balcony. Stainless steel railings also look great on all types of staircases

Curtain Wall Glazing

A curtain wall system is an outer covering of a building in which the outer walls are non-structural, utilized only to keep the weather out and the occupants in. Since the curtain wall is non-structural, it can be made of lightweight materials, thereby reducing construction costs.
